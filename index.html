About us

Built on Experience and Product Quality

Pulikkuth Enterprises manufactures samosa leaves with a focus on consistency, hygiene, and usability. The makers behind Ogito samosa leaves have over 9 years of experience in samosa leaf production. Pulikkuth Group started Pulikkuth Enterprises in 2019 to scale manufacturing while maintaining quality standards.

Manufactured with Care

Our samosa leaves are produced using a controlled process to ensure uniform thickness, strength, and ease of handling.

Designed for Commercial Use

Made to fold easily, seal properly, and hold fillings without tearing during frying.
